About Us

Shwipe is the premiere casual shoe browsing application that allows users to effortlessly view the newest shoes. It differentiates itself from other shopping apps by bridging the gap between marketing and social media. Shwipe sources its photos from Flickr, to bring you a dynamic range of pictures showcasing how shoes are used in people's lives. We believe that this keeps the shopping experience exciting for customers, and can give a more accurate representation of how these shoes will look when worn. The signup process is simple - just login with Facebook and you're good to go! What are you waiting for? Start Shwiping!
